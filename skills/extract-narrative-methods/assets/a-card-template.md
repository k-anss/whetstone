# A Card Template (Weak-Side Negotiation)

Candidate-layer output. A line = a weak/constrained actor still producing a
result through exchange, borrowed leverage, or cost transfer. If the six
questions (actor · dilemma · action · response · cost · result) don't close,
downgrade to an event — do not force the card.

```markdown
### 案例 A01：<one-line method-shaped title>

- 章节：<chapters within this chunk>
- 结果状态：已完成 | 阶段完成 | 进行中 | 失败/反噬
- 主体：<the constrained actor who makes the central move>
- 困境：<the constraint; why ordinary power doesn't work here>
- 对方：<counterpart, and any party that is only an info source, not a trading partner>
- 我方有什么：<what the focal actor controls / can offer>
- 我方要什么：<the wanted outcome>
- 我方愿意放弃什么：<concession actually surrendered>
- 对方有什么：<counterpart's resources>
- 对方要什么：<counterpart's want>
- 对方响应：<observed response — silence/fear is not agreement>
- 关键动作：<the verifiable decisive action>
- 成交/借力结构：用【lever】换取【obtained】 (or: borrowed authority X)
- 实际代价：<cost actually borne; not a non-cost dressed up>
- 信息/立场暴露：<what acting revealed>
- 遗留风险：<residual risk; what is NOT yet resolved>
- 最强替代解释：<strongest rival explanation for the central inference>
- 预期管理：标⑦〔主导/在场〕 | 不标⑦   (or your project's expectation-mgmt tag)
- 定位锚：<6–15 字、可在本块原文逐字 grep 到的短片段，仅用于证明不是编造>
- 关键原句：<表意用，可转述/截断，无需逐字；可留空>
- 方法信号：<a method SIGNAL, never a stable-method declaration>
```

`定位锚` is the only anti-fabrication field: keep it short and verbatim so the
validator can `grep` it. `关键原句` is for meaning and may be paraphrased — it is
not checked. If the anchor can't be found, the card is **marked** 疑似编造 for
human review, not rejected.

Separate **paid cost** (实际代价) from **exposure** (信息/立场暴露) and
**residual risk** (遗留风险) — never move a possible future consequence into the
paid-cost field. The 最强替代解释 line is mandatory whenever a central claim
rests on inference.
